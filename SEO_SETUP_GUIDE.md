## 📋 YOUR ACTION ITEMS

### STEP 1: Google Search Console Setup (5 minutes)

#### A. Create Property
1. Go to: **https://search.google.com/search-console**
2. Click **"Add Property"**
3. Choose **"URL prefix"**
4. Enter: `https://www.superfly-commerce.com`
5. Click **"Continue"**

#### B. Verify Ownership
Google will show multiple verification methods. Choose **"HTML tag"**:

1. Copy the verification meta tag (looks like):
   ```html
   <meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR_CODE_HERE" />
   ```

2. **Send me this verification code** and I'll add it to your site

3. After I add it and you redeploy, go back to Search Console and click **"Verify"**

#### C. Submit Sitemap
1. In Search Console, go to **"Sitemaps"** (left sidebar)
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**
